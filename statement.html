Copilot Statement

This project was created with the assistance of Github's built-in copilot chat. I rarely use Copilot to generate my work, but to explore it for the requirements of this assignment, I explored responses from the chatbot rather than using google forums like I normally would. Whenever I got stuck, I would ask the chat why I'm not getting my desired result and it would explain to me a possible solution as to how I could fix it. Something I miss about forum posts, though, like on stack overflow, is the greater detail into the context of which users explain their solutions and why they work. When creating my second endpoint, I noticed that the inline recommendations had strikingly accurate generations to what I was about to type. It was helpful to be able to quickly glance to make sure it's correct, then press tab to save a second each couple lines. If I had to estimate, I'd say that those inline recommendations had about an 80% accuracy rate after I had made my first function.
